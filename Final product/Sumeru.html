Sumeru - Flower God Dance

                "Kashin Birthday" means "celebrating the birth of a flower god."
                According to legend, during the festival to celebrate the birthday 
                of the dendro archon, the flower god would dance and the 
                Patisara flowers would bloom to the tune of her dance.
                And now, Nilou uses her dance to recreate the beautiful story within this myth.
